Piccid

for iOS and Android

Featured as a finalist in the Kendo UI app contest.

identify stuff....

Ever find yourself wondering what something is and needing to find out? This app is the answer.

Take photos of things you want to identify and upload them to the piccid service. Let other users help you work out what they are. Image posts and good comments are rewarded with points.

The free app is available for download through the Apple App Store and Google Play.
